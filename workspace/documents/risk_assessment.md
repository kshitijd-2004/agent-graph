# Risk Assessment

## Financial Risks
- Market volatility affecting Q4 projections
- Currency fluctuations in international markets
- Rising operational costs

## Operational Risks
- Supply chain delays (primary vendor unreliable)
- Resource constraints in engineering team
- Infrastructure scaling challenges

## Strategic Risks
- Competitor CompCorp launching competing product Q1
- Technology disruption from new AI paradigms
- Changing regulatory landscape (EU AI Act)

## Mitigation Plans
- Diversify vendor base (3 suppliers minimum)
- Cross-train engineering team
- Invest in cloud infrastructure
- Monitor regulatory changes weekly
